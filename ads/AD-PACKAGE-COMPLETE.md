# COMPLIANCE & LEGAL NOTES
# ═══════════════════════════════════════════

⚠️ **REQUIRED before running ads:**

1. **TCPA Compliance:** TCPA consent language is on every form. Verify with your attorney that it covers your specific use case.

2. **NMLS License:** If acting as a mortgage broker/lender, display your NMLS number on all pages. If a lead generation site connecting to licensed lenders, state that clearly.

3. **Google Ads:** Financial products require Google Financial Products certification. Apply at ads.google.com/home/tools/financial-products/

4. **Facebook Ads:** Housing ads have Special Ad Category restrictions. Must use "Special Ad Category: Housing" when setting up campaigns — disables age/zip targeting but enables housing lookalikes.

5. **Fair Housing:** All ads must comply with Fair Housing Act. No targeting or exclusion based on race, religion, national origin, sex, familial status, or disability.

6. **State Licensing:** Some states require specific disclosures for mortgage lead gen. Consult legal counsel.

7. **APR Disclosure:** If rates are mentioned in ads, ensure APR disclosure requirements are met.
